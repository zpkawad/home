Kawad Z.P. School

Our school is marathi medium government school. It is located in a tribal area of Thane district in Maharashtra state. Despite our school being located only 55 kms away from the financial capital of India i.e Mumbai, our school is lacking many modern educational facilities which are required for giving quality education for students.

The total enrollment of students in our school is 235. There are 1st to 8th classes in our school. As a Kendra school, a number of taluka and district level programs and sport competitions are held in our school.
