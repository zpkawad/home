Wablewadi Z.P. School

Can you imagine a Zilla Parishad school that was struggling to get students few years back, and had only 32 students, now has more than 550 enrolments of students with 3500 plus admissions on waiting list for next year? And can you also believe that parents around have withdrawn admissions from high profiled schools and CBSE like curriculum and opted this ZP school for their children? Can you imagine a school that had 2 class rooms, 2 teachers still producing amazing academic results? Can you believe that this school is recently declared as International School under OJAS International School Project of Government of Maharashtra? Can you believe that students here do not stay at home during vacations, they come to school, even today, and they go home late, voluntarily?
